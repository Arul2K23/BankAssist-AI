# BankAssist AI: Operations Engine
**Executive Dossier & Presentation Guide**

---

## 🏗️ 1. The Technology Stack
To provide professional-grade banking assistance, we used a specialized stack:

*   **Backend:** **FastAPI (Python 3.14)**. Designed for high-frequency banking queries.
*   **Neural Storage:** **Qdrant (The Secure Policy Vault)**. Uses mathematical vectors to map the complex relationships in banking regulations.
*   **The Brain:** **Google Gemini 2.0 Flash**. Chosen for its ability to analyze massive bank SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) in milliseconds.
*   **Frontend:** **React + Vite**. Premium banking interface with focus on clarity and speed.

---

## 🚀 2. Core Banking Features
1.  **Direct Policy Search (RAG):** The AI scans actual bank manuals before answering, ensuring every word is backed by authorized banking text.
2.  **Audit Ledger:** Tracks every query made by branch staff to ensure the AI's advice is compliant with regulatory standards.
3.  **Branch SOP Vault:** A specialized library where staff can manually browse authorized procedures across all departments.
4.  **Operational Memory:** Remembers recurring staff questions to ensure consistent branch protocols.

---

## 🔒 3. Banking Security & Validation
*   **Source Verification:** Every AI answer is grounded in a specific Bank Policy file.
*   **Hallucination Prevention:** If a banking procedure isn't found in the vault, the AI correctly identifies that it cannot provide an unauthorized answer.
*   **Access Control:** Branch managers see full operational audits; tellers see only what is necessary for their specific clearance level.
